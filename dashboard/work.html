Tier 2Flagship training plan v1 — the long arc

review229777d

A synthesis of four parallel lit-reviews + the data audit into a concrete 8-phase training recipe. EfficientNet-Lite0 backbone (unanimous across three reviews), cleanlab-first cleaning of the 34K weak AIY labels, decoupled two-stage training (Kang 2020) with logit adjustment, Hairy/Downy specialist head, and an energy-based OOD gate.

The hybrid that makes sense long-term: flagship handles the species it has strong training signal on; AIY is the fallback for rare-bird cases via the unknown trigger. As more nodes come online and more data accumulates, flagship's coverage expands and AIY's fallback role shrinks. Neither replaces the other — they complement.

Label set: 16 classes (14 species + not_a_bird + unknown) Targets: top-1 ≥75%, macro-F1 ≥65%, ECE ≤5%, OOD AUROC ≥0.85 Baseline to beat: AIY pre-Sonoma 69.3%, post-cleanup likely similar Phases: 0 (harness, done) → 1 (clean) → 2-4 (train) → 5 (OOD) → 6 (quantize) → 7 (shadow) → 8 (cutover) Doc: docs/superpowers/specs/2026-04-23-tier2-training-plan-v1.md
